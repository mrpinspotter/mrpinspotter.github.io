About Us

Precision Pinspotter Service, Inc. was founded in 1989. We specialize in the repair and maintenance of AMF Pinsetting Equipment. We valued our customers and are dedicated to repairing your equipment and preventing future problems.


We are sure you will be happy with our work! Some of the variety of services we offer include:

  • AMF Equipment Repairs
  • On-Site Service
  • Chassis Repair
  • Motors Service and Repair
